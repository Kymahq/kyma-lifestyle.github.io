Intent-aware product matching

A salaried lead asking for ₹50K and a self-employed lead asking for ₹50K aren't the same conversation. Kyma surfaces the right product — personal, business, top-up, balance transfer — based on what the borrower actually needs.

Cross-sell in context

Existing customers get top-ups, BT offers, and secured upgrades surfaced naturally — only when the conversation warrants it. Kyma references repayment history and prior products by name, the way a real DSA would.

Closes the application

RBI-aligned consent gate before any KYC pull. Structured KYC via WhatsApp Flows, not free-text. Soft retries on PAN and identity mismatches so a typo doesn't lose a lead. Clean handoff to a credit officer at finalization.
